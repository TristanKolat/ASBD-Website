About

Founded in 1988, A Sign By Design manufactures and installs commercial signage across Indiana and the nation. Our team of professionals applies its experience and expertise to assist clients in creating their unique corporate image with custom signage that becomes an integral part of their business success while meeting regulatory compliance.

We strive to become the number one Sign Company in Indiana as we custom design our signage to any client specifications, work in conjunction with local authorities & permitting offices, and offer excellent project management to ensure client satisfaction.

In over 35 years of operation, A Sign By Design’s signs have landscaped the city of Indianapolis. They identify the products and services that you use on a daily basis. At the airport, the RCA sign “Welcomes You to Indianapolis.” Community Health Network signage directs you to the emergency room or to your doctor’s office. Your view from your seat at Victory Field provides an illuminated roster of our clients; Courtyard by Marriott, Simon Properties, the Hoosier Lottery and Key Bank. We have expanded our operations by manufacturing and installing signs across the country.

Our Founder & President

In August of 1988, Beverly started A Sign By Design, Inc. with less than $2,000 of seed money in a bedroom of her home. It all began in a spare room with a telephone and a fax machine and a desire to succeed.

Beverly has overcome adversity, exhibited good business strategies, and has weathered three recessions over 35 years of business. Her retention of talented people reflects how she values her employees and their families. She has gone from being a single mother on food stamps, to being an employer of more than 35 talented people.

She recognized early on, in order to succeed in a predominately male industry, she would have to work harder and smarter. She is now recognized as one of the top women business owners and ranked number three in the commercial sign industry in Indianapolis. A Sign By Design is currently ranked third in the Indianapolis Business Journal’s Book of Lists in the sign category.

In 2009, the IBJ recognized A Sign By Design as one of the top 25 women owned businesses, a title that ASBD still owns to this day. Beverly has also received recognition by being named Blue Chip Business Award winner by Ball State University, Starkey Entrepreneurial Award and Woman Business Owner of the Year by the National Association of Woman Business Owners.

She continues to be a leader in the Indianapolis area as a successful woman business owner by being a role model for many aspiring women and a valued member to many businesses networking groups, including IndyCrew, NAWBO, IFMA, BOMA, IREM, Indy-REAL and more.
